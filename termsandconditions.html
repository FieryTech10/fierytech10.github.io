Investment Terms And Conditions

1. Investment Amount:

  • The investor agrees to invest the amount specified in the contract into the Company's cargo running operations.
  • Any payments outside of contract amount are not considered as your investment, so please ensure you send the correct amount!!

2. Use of Funds:

  • The Company commits to using the investment solely for cargo running operations.

3. Profit Generation And Distribution:

  • Profits generated from the cargo runs will be calculated and distributed as outlined in the contract.
  • The company retains some of the profit.
  • The investor will receive most of the profit.
  • Insurance is not returned but is worth the cost in case of loss.

4. Risk Acknowledgment:

  • The Investor acknowledges that cargo running in Star Citizen involves inherent risks, including but not limited to in-game piracy, ship damage, or loss.
  • The Company is not liable for any losses incurred due to these risks.
  • If insurance has been purchased, the investor will receive their original investment back in case of loss.

5. Reporting and Communication:

  • The Company will provide updates on the status of the cargo runs and the profits generated upon request.
  • The Investor has the right to request additional information regarding their investment.

6. Withdrawal and Termination:

  • The Investor may withdraw their investment under the following conditions:
  • 1. The cargo has not yet been purchased.
  • 2. The company has lost the cargo and the investor has insurance.
  • 3. The company decides to return your investment early for whatever reason.

7. Confidentiality:

  • Both parties agree to keep all terms of the investment and details of the cargo runs confidential. Leaked details may cause loss of cargo due to pirates.
  • The company retains the right to not provide the investor with the route of trade.

8. Resets And Wipes (OOC t&c)

  • We are not liable to loss through wipes, if there has been an aUEC wipe/reset due to an update, there will be no return for your investment.

Cruises & Tours Terms Of Service

1. Booking and Payment:

  • All bookings must be made through the official Luminalia Investment Fund portal.
  • Full payment is required at the time of booking to secure your reservation.
  • Accepted payment methods include UEC and other approved in-game currencies.

2. Cancellations and Refunds:

  • Cancellations made more than 3 hours prior to the departure date will receive a full refund.
  • Cancellations made within 3 hours of the departure date are non-refundable.
  • In the event of a cruise or tour cancellation by Luminalia Investment Fund, a full refund will be issued to all affected passengers, even if within 3 hours.
  • If there is a reschedule and the difference is over 1 hour, the customer will have an option to refund or keep their booking. If no reply is made to a reschedule notice, the booking will assume to be kept.

3. Travel Documentation:
