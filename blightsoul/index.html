Info:

Blightsoul is a medieval-themed text based idle game set in a world filled with magic and adventure. The player takes on the role of a young entrepreneur seeking to create their own business in this fantasy world. As they journey through the land, they will encounter various challenges and opportunities to grow their business, from trading goods with merchants to battling fierce monsters for valuable treasures. Along the way, players will also encounter a cast of colorful characters, each with their own unique stories and quests to complete. With its immersive world and rich gameplay, Blightsoul is sure to keep players engaged for hours on end.
