What is Luminalia Investment Fund?

Luminalia Investment Fund is a financial organization dedicated to providing high-return investment opportunities. We also offer a membership program and flights & tours aswell, to give our investors the most enjoyable experience possible.

How do I invest with Luminalia Investment Fund?

Investing with us is easy! Simply visit our Investments page, choose the type of investment you're interested in, and follow the instructions to complete your investment. Our standard waiting time is 1-3 days, but may be less. If you have any questions, our support team is here to help.

What are the risks involved in investing?

While all investments carry some level of risk, Luminalia Investment Fund strives to minimize these risks through careful planning and expert management. We offer various investment durations to cater to different risk appetites, with longer investments providing higher returns. We offer insurance so in the case of loss, you will still get your investment back and we will cover the lost amount as if nothing happened! Some of the various risks may include possible pirate action, ship malfunction/crash and other various means.

What returns can I expect from my investment?

The returns on your investment depend on the type and duration of the investment. We offer competitive returns, with up to 30% ROI on certain investment plans. For detailed information and estimated return, please visit our Investments page.

How do I track my investments?

Once you've made an investment, you can track its progress by contacting the staff member that originally provided you with the contract. They will provide you with information such as how much your investment has made to date and how much longer is estimated to be left. You will be contacted upon receiving your investment return with a photo as proof of funds being transfered. If you need further assistance, our support team is available to help.

Can I withdraw my investment early?

Unfortunately, you will not be able to withdraw your investment early. All calculations are done prior to investment, to make it easiest on our team you will need to wait for return. The only exception is if for whatever reason your order cannot be completed, you will receive your investment along with anything made with it.

How do I apply to become a member?

After you have completed 2 contracts with us (either as pilot or investor) you can contact a staff member to apply as a member. A detailed invoice will be provided which must be paid in full and after payment you will receive exclusive benefits ranging from cheaper insurance, discounted tours/cruises and more!. For more information please view our Membership Page

How can I contact Luminalia Investment Fund?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please visit our Contact Us page. Our support team is ready to help you with any inquiries you may have.
