How To Become A Member

Becoming a member is extremely simple! All you need to do is have at least 2 successful contracts with us, wether you're a pilot or investor. A contract is considered "completed" when the ending terms are met, which is commonly after the investor has received their return payment.

After conditions are met, simply contact a staff member and request to be upgraded to member, in which you will be sent an invoice detailing how much it will cost to purchase membership. Costs may vary but our general price is 499,999 UEC. This will be a one-time payment that will quickly make your money back, plus more!
